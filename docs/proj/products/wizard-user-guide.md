## Getting Started

To start creating a new product:

1. Navigate to the Admin Dashboard
2. Click on "Products" in the main navigation
3. Click the "Add New Product" button
4. You'll be directed to the new Product Wizard interface

> **Tip**: You can save your progress at any time by clicking the "Save as Draft" button in the bottom-left corner.

## Step 1: Basic Information

In this step, you'll enter the essential details about your product:

![Basic Information Step](../../../client/src/assets/docs/wizard-step1.png)

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Product Name | The main title of your product | Yes |
| Slug | URL-friendly version of the name (auto-generated but editable) | Yes |
| Category | The product category | Yes |
| Price | Regular selling price | Yes |
| Cost Price | Your cost to acquire the product | No |
| Brand | Product brand name | No |
| Description | Detailed product description with rich text editor | No |

> **Tip**: A well-written description with relevant keywords will improve your product's discoverability in search results.

## Step 2: Product Images

Upload and manage your product images in this step:

![Product Images Step](../../../client/src/assets/docs/wizard-step2.png)

### Image Requirements:
- Minimum resolution: 800x800 pixels
- Maximum file size: 5MB
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP

### Actions:
- **Upload**: Click the upload area or drag and drop images
- **Reorder**: Drag images to change their order (first image will be the main product image)
- **Delete**: Hover over an image and click the trash icon
- **Edit**: Click on an image to access cropping and enhancement tools

> **Tip**: High-quality images from multiple angles increase conversion rates significantly.

## Step 3: Additional Information

Add detailed specifications and attributes to your product:

![Additional Information Step](../../../client/src/assets/docs/wizard-step3.png)

### Sections:
1. **General Attributes**: Weight, dimensions, materials, etc.
2. **Category-Specific Attributes**: Attributes relevant to the selected category
3. **Custom Attributes**: Create custom specifications for your product

### AI Assistance:
- Click "Suggest Attributes" to get AI-powered attribute recommendations
- The system will analyze your product details and images to suggest relevant attributes

> **Tip**: Complete attributes improve product filtering and search functionality, helping customers find your products more easily.

## Step 4: Review & Submit

Review all product information before final submission:

![Review & Submit Step](../../../client/src/assets/docs/wizard-step4.png)

### Actions:
- Review each section by expanding the accordion panels
- Make last-minute edits by clicking the "Edit" button in each section
- Submit your product by clicking "Publish Product"
- Or, save as a draft to continue later

### Publishing Options:
- **Publish Now**: Make the product immediately available
- **Schedule**: Set a future date and time for the product to become available
- **Save as Draft**: Continue working on the product later

> **Tip**: Use the "Preview" button to see how your product will appear to customers before publishing.

## Working with Drafts

The system automatically saves your progress, but you can also manually save drafts:

### Managing Drafts:
1. Access your drafts from the Products > Drafts menu
2. Each draft shows the last edited date and completion status
3. Continue editing by clicking on any draft
4. Delete unwanted drafts using the "Delete" action

> **Tip**: You can have multiple drafts for different products in progress simultaneously.

## Troubleshooting

Common issues and their solutions:

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Image upload fails | Check that your image meets the size and format requirements |
| Can't proceed to next step | Look for error messages on required fields that might be incomplete |
| Draft not saving | Check your internet connection and try manually saving |
| Product not appearing in store | Verify that the product status is set to "Published" and not "Draft" |
